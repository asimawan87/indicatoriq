Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Trading Indicator IQ ("the App") is developed by TriNoor Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • No personal data (name, email, location) is collected.
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting services are used.
  • No advertising networks are integrated.
  • No data is sent to any server.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device to provide its features:

  • Bookmarks: Indicators and patterns you save are stored in your device's local database (Room).
  • Quiz progress: Your quiz scores and answers are stored locally.
  • App preferences: Settings such as theme and sort order are stored in your device's local storage (DataStore).

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

3. Internet Permission

The App does not request internet permission and makes no network connections of any kind.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of the App change, this page will be updated. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the revision date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: trinoor.apps@gmail.com
